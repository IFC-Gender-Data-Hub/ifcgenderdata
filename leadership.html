Overview

Women’s representation in leadership remains uneven across firms and markets. While progress is visible in some regions, gaps persist in both board and senior management roles.

Women in leadership positions

Firms with higher representation of women in leadership tend to show stronger governance outcomes, yet many firms remain below key thresholds such as 30 percent representation.

Why it matters

Leadership diversity is linked to decision-making quality, organizational performance, and broader inclusion outcomes within firms.
